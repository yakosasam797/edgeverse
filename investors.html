Investment Thesis

Why EdgeVerse. Why now.

01

Massive Unserved Market

220M+ two-wheelers in India with zero ADAS/ARAS coverage. India's road fatality crisis demands an immediate, technology-driven solution. The regulatory tailwind from BIS mandates is accelerating adoption.

02

Deep Technology Moat

Vertically integrated hardware + software + AI. Patented sensor fusion architecture. India Perception Model trained on uniquely Indian road conditions — chaotic traffic, no lane markings, mixed vehicle types.

03

Platform Expansion Play

The same Imedge® + Perceiva™ stack powers three trillion-dollar verticals: mobility ARAS, smart surveillance, and industrial automation. One platform, multiple massive revenue streams.

04

World-Class Founding Team

Founded by engineers with deep expertise in embedded systems, computer vision, and automotive technology. Advised by veterans from Valeo, Ambarella, Qualcomm, and BIS.

05

Regulatory Tailwind

India's Bureau of Indian Standards is actively mandating ADAS features for vehicles. EdgeVerse is positioned as the category-defining solution when these mandates take effect for two-wheelers.

06

Capital-Efficient Model

Asset-light hardware design leveraging existing silicon (Ambarella CV25). Software-first revenue model with recurring OEM licensing and fleet management subscriptions.
