Detection Pipeline

From perception to protection.

A four-stage pipeline that turns raw sensor data into real-time safety intelligence.

01
Capture
150° FoV HDR camera + RADAR + IMU continuously stream environmental data at up to 30 FPS.
02
Perceive
Perceiva™ CNN stack classifies vehicles, pedestrians, lanes, and hazards using India Perception Model.
03
Predict
Multi-zone collision prediction evaluates threat trajectory, closing speed, and time-to-impact in real-time.
04
Alert
Haptic, visual, or audio alerts delivered to the rider in under 1 second — with automatic video freeze.
