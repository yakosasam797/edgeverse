Perceiva™ ARAS Platform

Advanced AI technology for safer, smarter riding.

Enabling OEMs and cluster makers to unlock all levels of two-wheeler rider assistance at scale.

Schedule a demo →

Perceiva™ ARAS software learns to perceive using data, equipping vehicles with advanced human-like awareness. Designed for safety, it adapts to unexpected and unseen situations across India's most challenging roads.

01 Forward Collision Warning

See what's ahead. React before it's too late.

Multi-sensor fusion detects vehicles, pedestrians, and obstacles up to 100 meters ahead. The system alerts the rider with visual and haptic warnings before a collision becomes inevitable.

100mDetection range
<100msAlert latency
02 Blind Spot Detection

360° awareness for every lane change.

Peripheral perception monitors adjacent lanes and blind zones. When a vehicle enters the danger zone, the system provides instant directional alerts to prevent side-swipe collisions.

170°Field of view
24/7Day & night operation
03 Lane Departure Warning

Stay in lane. Stay alive.

Even on Indian roads where lane markings are faded or non-existent, Perceiva™ infers road boundaries using AI-learned road geometry and alerts riders when they drift dangerously.

No MapsRequired for operation
Real-TimeRoad boundary inference
04 Adaptive Speed Advisory

Context-aware speed intelligence.

The system analyses road type, traffic density, weather conditions, and surrounding vehicle speeds to provide real-time speed advisories — reducing over-speeding by up to 40%.

30 FPSContinuous analysis
40%Speed violation reduction

Built for every deployment model

From OEM integration to fleet management, Perceiva™ scales across every use case in the two-wheeler ecosystem.

OEM Integration

White-label Perceiva™ SDK for direct integration into instrument clusters and ECUs. Pre-certified for BIS compliance with plug-and-play OEM APIs.

Fleet Management

Cloud-connected fleet intelligence with real-time driver behaviour scoring, route safety analytics, and predictive risk assessment for commercial fleets.

Aftermarket Retrofit

Standalone Imedge® device for retrofitting existing two-wheelers. Universal mount system compatible with 95% of India's two-wheeler fleet. Install in under 30 minutes.
