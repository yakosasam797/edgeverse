Hardware Platform

Imedge® — Real-Time AI Vision Hardware

Imedge™ brings the power of high-performance computer vision directly to the source of data. Powered by the Ambarella CV25 SoC with its dedicated CVflow® architecture, it delivers industry-leading AI performance-per-watt.

Ambarella CV25 SoC with dedicated CVflow® architecture
Arm® 64-bit quad core processor
Onsemi AR0147AT HDR Camera · 150° FoV
4KP30+ encoding · H.264/H.265 recording
Industry interfaces — WiFi, BT5.2, HDMI, GPS/GNSS, 6-Axis IMU
Customizable form factor for automotive & industrial environments
AI Perception Stack

Perceiva™ — AI Vision + Radar Perception Platform

A layered suite of software components designed for real-time, sensor-based applications directly on edge devices. Specifically engineered for the constraints of edge hardware, this modular architecture ensures efficient execution of complex computer vision and radar tasks.

Sensor Fusion Engine — Camera + RADAR + IMU data fusion
India Perception Model — Trained on Indian road conditions
Object Detection & Classification — Real-time at 30 FPS
ISP + HDR Merge — Superior low-light processing
Linux BSP — Low-level drivers, bootloader, kernel configurations
Privacy-First — All processing on-device, zero cloud dependency
